## Overview

This data policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website.

## Data Collection

### Automatically Collected Data

When you visit our website, our server automatically records certain information:

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Referring website
- Date and time of access
- Pages visited

### Cookies

We use essential cookies to ensure the proper functioning of our website. These cookies do not collect personal information.

## Data Usage

We use collected data solely for:

- Providing and maintaining our website
- Analyzing website usage to improve user experience
- Ensuring website security

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

- When required by law
- To protect our rights and property
- With your explicit consent

## Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to data processing
- Data portability

## Contact

For questions about this data policy or to exercise your rights, contact us at:
Email: privacy@hololux.com

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this data policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
